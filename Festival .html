Sankranthi

Makar Sankranti, Sankranti or Sankranthi is one of the main festivals in India. It is the first festival of the year in the Gregorian calendar. This festival is celebrated by the people all over India. It marks the transition of the sun into Makara rashi (Capricorn) on its path across the sky. It is celebrated on the 14th or 15th of January. Makar Sankranti is celebrated with social festivities such as colourful decorations, melas(fairs), bonfires and feasts. Makar Sankranti is an important pan-Indian solar festival, known by different names , though observed on the same date. It is known as Pedda Panduga in Telengana, Makara Sankranti in Karnataka, and by other names.
